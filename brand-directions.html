Brand Directions for Gather

Four concepts for how the brand could look and feel. Each keeps the Gather red as an anchor but takes the identity in a different direction. Pick the one that resonates and we'll incorporate it across the site.

Direction A

Warm Sunset

Organic, inviting, like golden hour at a gathering. Feels like the warmth of being welcomed somewhere.

Gather Red
Amber
Gold
Honey
Cream
A warm gradient that flows from Gather's signature red through amber and gold. Applied to accent elements, section dividers, and CTAs. The overall palette feels like a sunset — inviting, communal, optimistic. Typography stays clean and modern but the color story says "come as you are."
Gay Men London · Sundays

Mood: Airbnb meets sunset yoga retreat. Approachable luxury.

Direction B

Electric Community

Vibrant, alive, energetic. The feeling of a room full of people who are all genuinely happy to be there.

Gather Red
Magenta
Violet
Indigo
Lavender
An energetic gradient that sweeps from Gather red through magenta and violet. Bold, expressive, youthful — but still refined. This direction leans into the idea that Gather is for people who want more from their social life. Not quiet desperation — active, joyful intention. The gradient could animate subtly on key sections.
Queer Women Brooklyn · Sundays

Mood: Spotify Wrapped meets Instagram Stories. Fun, expressive, unmistakably modern.

Direction C

Earth & Light

Grounded, intentional, like a community garden. Depth without heaviness, nature without cliche.

Forest
Sage
Mint
Fern
Moss
A nature-inspired palette grounded in forest greens and sage. Gather's red becomes the accent that pops against the calm — like a campfire in the woods. This direction communicates depth, authenticity, and the idea that real connection is an organic thing that grows over time. Elevated but not sterile.
Outdoors New York · Saturdays

Mood: Patagonia meets Headspace. Calm confidence, rooted in something real.

Direction D

Minimal Luxe

Restrained elegance. Let the content and the people do the talking. The brand disappears so the experience shines.

Ink
Charcoal
Gather Red
Off-white
White
Almost monochrome — just Gather red as the single pop of color. Maximum restraint, maximum confidence. This is the Apple approach: the brand is so sure of itself it doesn't need to shout. Typography, whitespace, and photography do the heavy lifting. The gradient is used sparingly — a thin line, a subtle glow — never a splashy background.
Artists New York · Wednesdays

Mood: Apple.com meets Aesop. Premium restraint. Less is everything.
